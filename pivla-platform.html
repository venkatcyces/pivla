Features

Everything you need for airtight compliance

Four powerful modules working in concert — from real-time wage tracking to AI-powered compliance guidance.

Command Center
  • Real-time compliance dashboard: Monitor apprentice ratios, wage determinations, and projects across multiple sites with live updates
  • Custom alert configurations: Receive instant notifications for potential compliance gaps or documentation needs
  • Multi-project overview: Manage IRA tax credit eligibility and PW&A requirements across concurrent projects
Smart Wage Calculator
  • Automatic rate updates: Sync with federal/state databases (DOL, SAM.gov) for always-accurate wage determinations
  • Multi-jurisdiction support: Handle varying requirements across all 50 states and federal projects
  • Overtime/holiday pay calculations: Automatically apply complex pay rules with audit-ready documentation
Report Wizard
  • One-click report generation: Produce IRS-ready documentation for tax credit claims and audits
  • Customizable templates: Create branded reports with project-specific compliance metrics
  • Audit-trail documentation: Maintain encrypted records for the full IRS recapture period
AI Assistant
  • Natural language query: Get clear answers to your compliance questions in plain English
  • Instant regulatory guidance: Step-by-step checklists for IRA tax credit optimization
  • Predictive compliance: Machine learning identifies and forecasts potential audit risks 30–60 days in advance
